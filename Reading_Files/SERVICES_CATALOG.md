## 📋 Services Overview by Category

### 1. 🦷 Preventive Dentistry
*Focus: Prevention and early detection*

1. General Examination - Routine check-ups and diagnostic evaluations
2. Professional Cleanings - Removal of plaque and tartar (prophylaxis)
3. Digital X-Rays & 3D Imaging - Detailed scanning for hidden issues
4. Dental Sealants - Protective coatings on chewing surfaces
5. Fluoride Treatments - Strengthening enamel to prevent cavities
6. Oral Cancer Screening - Early detection of oral cancer
7. Sports Mouthguards & Nightguards - Custom-fitted preventive devices

### 2. 🔧 Restorative Dentistry
*Focus: Restoring function and structure*

1. Dental Fillings - Tooth-colored composite resins for cavities
2. Inlays & Onlays - Conservative restorations between fillings and crowns
3. Crowns & Bridges - Caps for damaged teeth and artificial teeth
4. Dentures - Full or partial removable sets for missing teeth
5. Root Canal Therapy (Endodontics) - Treatment for infected tooth pulp
6. Dental Implants - Permanent surgical replacements for missing teeth
7. Simple Tooth Extraction - Basic extraction of loose or non-surgical teeth

### 3. ✨ Cosmetic Dentistry
*Focus: Aesthetic enhancement*

1. Teeth Whitening - Professional bleaching (in-office or take-home kits)
2. Porcelain Veneers - Thin custom-made shells for front teeth
3. Dental Bonding - Tooth-colored resin application for repairs
4. Gum Contouring - Reshaping the gumline for aesthetic purposes
5. Smile Makeovers - Comprehensive customized cosmetic procedures

### 4. 🦷➡️ Orthodontics
*Focus: Teeth and bite alignment*

1. Traditional Braces - Metal or ceramic brackets and wires
2. Clear Aligners - Invisible aligner trays (like Invisalign)
3. Retainers - Custom devices to maintain teeth position

### 5. 🫶 Periodontics (Gum Disease Treatment)
*Focus: Gum and bone health*

1. Scaling and Root Planing - Deep cleaning to treat gum disease
2. Gum Grafting - Surgery to treat receding gums
3. Periodontal Maintenance - Ongoing specialized cleanings

### 6. 🏥 Specialty & Additional Services
*Focus: Complex and specialized care*

1. Oral Surgery - Complex extractions and bone grafting
2. Emergency Dentistry - Urgent care for severe toothaches and trauma
3. TMJ Therapy - Treatments for jaw joint pain and disorders
4. Sleep Apnea/Snoring Appliances - Custom mouthguards for airway opening

### 7. 👶 Pediatric Dentistry
*Focus: Children's dental care*

1. Children's Dental Exams - Age-appropriate check-ups
2. Children's Cleanings - Gentle preventive cleaning for kids
3. Fluoride Treatments for Children - Topical fluoride for growing teeth
4. Sealants for Kids - Protective coatings on permanent molars

### 8. 📞 Consultation Services
*Focus: Assessment and treatment planning*

1. Free 5-Minute Consultation - Quick assessment with dentist
2. 1-Hour Comprehensive Consultation - In-depth examination and treatment plan
